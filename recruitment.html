I Requirements

Straightforward and fair. Meet these and you'll thrive in the main roster.

Minimum Power

Main [BRO]: 60M+ power and Truegold 1 (TG1) to join.
Academies: no minimum — all growing players welcome.

Activity

Daily login, alliance help & gifts, and participation in both Bear Trap phases when you're online. Shield up before going inactive.

Communication

Day-to-day coordination runs in-game. Discord is optional — used mainly for KvK castle battles and Tri-Alliance events, where raid leads will be on voice.

What we expect of every member

  • Show up for Bear Trap — BT1 at UTC 08:00, BT2 at UTC 13:00, every second day
  • Be reachable in-game — rally calls and event timing run in alliance chat
  • Daily login for gifts, help requests, and your own progression
  • Read the Bear Trap guide — and apply it (correct hero in slot 1, every time)
  • Shield up before going inactive — protect your account and the alliance
  • No drama in alliance chat — disagreements go to DM with R4/R5
  • Respect every member — F2P or whale, everyone contributes

II Why Brotherhood

We're not just a flag. We're a coordinated order with written playbooks, voice during events, and members who actually help each other.

Written Guides

Every major event has a step-by-step playbook tailored to our server. Stop guessing — start optimizing.

Live Event Calls

In-game rally and timing calls for every event. Voice on Discord for the big ones — KvK castle battles and Tri-Alliance — led by our raid leads.

Active Defense

R4/R5 monitor for incoming raids. You'll get reinforcements and shield pings before you take damage.

Full Tech & Gifts

Maxed alliance research and consistent gift tiers from coordinated event play.

Helpful Chat

Ask a question, get an answer. Veteran members actually share what they know.

Progression Coaching

F2P, low-spend, or whale — leadership helps you plan the next 30 days of your account.

III The Academies

Newer or smaller? Start in an academy. Grow, learn the playbooks, and earn your place in the main roster.

[bro] BROacademy

First Academy

The primary feeder alliance. Learn our Bear Trap protocol, build toward main-roster power, and get hands-on coaching from veteran members. No power minimum — just bring activity and willingness to learn.

[bra] BroAcademy

Second Academy

Our overflow and growth alliance for newer players and alts. Same coordinated culture, same access to our guides, with a relaxed entry bar while you develop.

Not sure which fits? Apply anyway — leadership will place you in the right alliance based on your power and goals. You can always graduate up to [BRO] Brotherhood as you grow.
